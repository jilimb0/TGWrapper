# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate these standards.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it through repository contact channels. Reports will be reviewed and handled with care.
